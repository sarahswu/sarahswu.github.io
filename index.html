GitHub

MedSearch is website that incorporates a symptom checker to cater to the needs of people to receive help with mental and physical ailments during a time like Covid-19 in which people cannot see a health professional. The website gives people the ability to unofficially self-diagnose their physical sicknesses without risking exposure to Covid-19 as well as provides resources to help with those dealing with stress or loneliness from isolation.

I created MedSearch with a team in 36 hours for HackDavis 2021. I worked on the design of the website as well as the front-end coding using Figma, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
